We Are

A team of 12 to 18 years old students, helped by mentors, participating in the First Robotics Competition.


Our Mission

Team PLS 5618 wants to give to students an enriching and professional experience to learn knowledge about technology in a climate that favors confidence and autonomy.
The project stimulates the interest in sciences and technology while developping a sense of belonging for the team, the school and the community.
