We had to use our perseverence and determination to find sponsors and to build a robot that worked well, which allowed us to reach 5th place and win the highest rookie seed award at the Montreal Regional.


Theme: Recycle Rush

Recycle Rush is played by two Alliances of three Teams each. Alliances compete simultaneously to score points by stacking Totes on Scoring Platforms, capping those stacks with Recycling Containers, and properly disposing of Litter, represented by pool noodles, in designated locations. In keeping with the recycling theme of the game, all scoring elements used are reusable or recyclable by teams in their home locations or by FIRST at the end of the season.
