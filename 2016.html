2016 Season

It's with a lot of perseverence and determination the we were able to conceive, finance and build a simple, reliable and performing robot. At the Montreal Regional, we were astonished by our performance since we were the winning alliance's captain and went to the Saint-Louis Chamionship.


Theme: Stronghold

Stronghold is played by two alliances of three teams each. Alliances compete against each other to breach their opponents’ defenses, known as outer works, and capture their tower. They score points by crossing elements of their opponents’ outer works, scoring boulders in their opponents’ tower goals, and surrounding and scaling their opponents’ tower itself.
